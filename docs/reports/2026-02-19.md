### 3. OpenAI EVMbench ‚Äî AI for Smart Contract Security üîê
**Score: 7.5/10** | New benchmark, shows Codex 5.3 capabilities

OpenAI and Paradigm launched EVMbench: 120 curated vulnerabilities from 40 audits, testing AI agents on three modes ‚Äî detect, patch, and exploit smart contract vulnerabilities. Key result: GPT-5.3-Codex scores 72.2% on exploit mode vs. GPT-5's 31.9% from six months ago.

**Interesting insight from the benchmark:** Agents are best at *exploiting* (clear objective: drain funds) but weaker at *detecting* (tend to stop after finding one issue) and *patching* (maintaining functionality while removing bugs is hard). This pattern likely generalizes to other coding agent tasks.

**Source:** [openai.com/index/introducing-evmbench](https://openai.com/index/introducing-evmbench/) | [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/tech/2026/02/18/sam-altman-s-openai-unveils-evmbench-to-test-whether-ai-can-keep-crypto-s-smart-contracts-safe)
