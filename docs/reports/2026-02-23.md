Treats LLM-based reasoning as an SMT theory ‚Äî represents documents as natural language text constraints, combines LLM evaluation with satisfiability modulo theory solving. First system extending neurosymbolic methods beyond fully formalizable domains.

### OMAD: Online Multi-Agent Diffusion Policies (arXiv 2602.18291)
First framework applying diffusion models to online multi-agent RL. Proposes relaxed policy objective that maximizes scaled joint entropy without tractable likelihood ‚Äî enabling exploration and coordination with diffusion's expressiveness.
